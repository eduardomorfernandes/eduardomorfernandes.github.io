About Me

I am a technology leader with 14 years of experience delivering reliable, scalable, and maintainable software systems across Brazil, Canada, and Europe. My background blends hands-on engineering with team leadership, having guided development teams of 5–10+ engineers and mentored more than 30 researchers and interns. I enjoy working at the intersection of architecture, backend engineering, DevOps, and AI/ML—ensuring that technical execution aligns with product and business needs.

Throughout my career, I’ve contributed to over ten industry-grade software products for organizations such as Petrobras, GOVBR, SUS-MS, and international research partners. I specialize in modernizing legacy applications, improving CI/CD workflows, building cloud-native systems, and introducing automation that strengthens software quality and accelerates delivery. My applied research in AI-enhanced development pipelines also gained attention from Apache and Red Hat engineering communities.

As a leader, I value clarity, collaboration, and pragmatic decision-making. I’m committed to creating an environment where engineers can grow, teams can move efficiently, and products can be delivered with high technical standards. My approach balances technical depth with people-centered leadership, enabling teams to execute effectively while fostering long-term engineering excellence.
